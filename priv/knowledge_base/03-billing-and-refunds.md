# Billing & refunds

This article covers payment methods, your billing cycle, seat changes, invoices, and our refund policy. All prices are in **US dollars** and exclude any VAT or sales tax that may apply in your region.

## Payment methods

We accept major **credit and debit cards** (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and **PayPal**. Annual plans of **20 seats or more** can also pay by **invoice / bank transfer** — contact **billing@beacon.example** to set this up.

## Your billing cycle

- Paid plans renew automatically — **monthly** or **annually**, depending on what you chose.
- Annual billing is discounted ~17% versus paying monthly.
- Your renewal date and next charge are shown under **Settings → Billing**.

## Adding or removing seats

- **Adding seats** takes effect immediately and is **prorated** for the rest of the current cycle.
- **Removing seats** takes effect on your **next** invoice. On monthly plans, removed seats are not refunded mid-cycle. On annual plans, the unused portion of removed seats is added to your account as **credit** toward future invoices.

## Upgrades and downgrades

- **Upgrading** (e.g., Pro → Business) applies immediately and is prorated.
- **Downgrading** applies at the end of the current billing cycle, so you keep the higher plan's features until then.

## Invoices and receipts

Every payment generates a receipt, emailed to your **billing contact** and available any time under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. You can add a company name, address, and VAT/tax ID there so they appear on your invoices.

## Refund policy

- **14-day money-back guarantee** on your **first** paid invoice (monthly or annual). If Beacon isn't right for you, contact us within 14 days of that first charge for a full refund.
- **After 14 days:**
  - **Monthly plans** are non-refundable, but you can cancel any time to stop the next charge — you keep access until the end of the current cycle.
  - **Annual plans** can be cancelled for **account credit** covering the unused full months remaining; this credit applies to future Beacon invoices.

## Failed payments

If a charge fails, we retry up to **3 times over 7 days** and email your billing contact. If payment still hasn't gone through after 7 days, the workspace becomes **read-only** until billing is resolved. Your data is retained — see **Data & integrations** for retention details.

## Cancelling

Cancel any time from **Settings → Billing → Cancel plan**. Cancelling stops future renewals; your workspace moves to the **Free** plan at the end of the paid period (data beyond Free limits is handled as described in **Data & integrations**).
