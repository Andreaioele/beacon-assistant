# Data & integrations

## Exporting your data

- Any **member** can export their projects to **CSV** from a project's **⋯ → Export**.
- **Admins** can export the **entire workspace** as **JSON** from **Settings → Export**.
- Exports are prepared in the background; we email a **download link valid for 24 hours** when it's ready.

## Importing data

You can import into Beacon from **CSV**, or directly from **Trello** or **Asana** using the importer at **Settings → Import**.

## Integrations

Beacon integrates with:

- **Slack** — notifications, and creating tasks from messages
- **GitHub** and **GitLab** — link commits and pull/merge requests to tasks
- **Google Calendar** — sync due dates
- **Google Drive** — attach files
- **Zapier** — connect to thousands of other apps

The **Free** plan includes **1** integration; **Pro** and **Business** include **all** integrations.

## API and webhooks

- A **REST API** is available on **Pro and Business**. Create a **personal access token** under **Settings → Developer**.
- Rate limits: **1,000 requests/hour** on Pro, **5,000 requests/hour** on Business. The Free plan has no API access.
- **Webhooks** (real-time event notifications to your endpoint) are available on the **Business** plan.

## Data retention after cancellation

- If you cancel or downgrade to **Free**, your workspace keeps everything that fits within Free limits. Projects beyond the Free limit of **3** become **read-only / archived** for **60 days**, giving you time to export or re-subscribe.
- If you **delete** a workspace, all data is permanently purged within **30 days** of the end of the grace period.

## Privacy and GDPR

- You can request export or deletion of personal data at **privacy@beacon.example**.
- A **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** is available for Business-plan customers.
