# Plans & pricing

Beacon has three plans: **Free**, **Pro**, and **Business**. Paid plans are billed **per member**, monthly or annually. Choosing annual billing saves about **17%** (you pay for 10 months instead of 12).

## Free trial

You can try **Pro or Business free for 14 days** — no credit card required. When the trial ends, your workspace automatically moves to the **Free** plan unless you choose a paid plan. Nothing is charged unless you actively subscribe.

## Changing plans

- You can **upgrade or downgrade at any time** from **Settings → Billing**.
- **Upgrades** take effect immediately.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

See **Billing & refunds** for how proration and seat changes are handled.

## A note on guests

**Guests** are people outside your team whom you invite to specific projects (for example, a client or a contractor). **Guests are free — they do not count as billable members** — but guest availability depends on your plan: none on Free, up to 10 per project on Pro, and unlimited on Business. Guests can only see the projects they're invited to, and can never access billing or workspace settings.
