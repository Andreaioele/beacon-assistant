# Getting started with Beacon

Beacon is a project and task management tool for small and growing teams. This article covers the basics: creating your workspace, your first project, and inviting your team.

> **Note:** *Beacon* is a fictional product used only for this exercise. The details below are internally consistent but invented.

## Creating your account

1. Go to **app.beacon.example** and choose **Sign up**.
2. Enter your work email and a password, or continue with Google.
3. Verify your email via the link we send you (valid for 24 hours).
4. Name your **workspace** — the shared home for your team's projects.

You start on the **Free** plan automatically. No credit card is required to sign up.

## Your first project

1. From the workspace home, click **New project** and give it a name.
2. Add **tasks** to the project. Each task can have an assignee, a due date, a description, and labels.
3. Switch between **List**, **Board** (kanban), and — on paid plans — **Timeline** (Gantt) views using the toggle at the top of the project.

## Inviting your team

1. Open **Members** from the workspace sidebar.
2. Click **Invite** and enter email addresses.
3. Choose a role for each invitee (see *Roles and permissions* in the **Account & security** article).

The Free plan supports up to **5 members**. To add more, upgrade to Pro or Business (see **Plans & pricing**).

## Mobile apps

Beacon has native apps for **iOS and Android**, available on the App Store and Google Play. The mobile apps let you view and edit tasks, comments, and notifications, and they work offline — your changes sync when you reconnect.

## Getting help

- Browse this help center.

- Free-plan users can ask questions in the community forum.
